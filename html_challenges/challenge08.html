American Breakfast Pancakes

  • Time: 40-60 minutes
  • Serves: 16 pancakes
Ingredients Quantity
Baking powder 2Â½ teaspoons
Salt 1 pinch
White sugar 1 teaspoon
Eggs (large and beaten) 2
Butter (melted and cooled) 30 grams + some for frying
Milk 300 millilitres
Plain flour 225 grams

Step 1: The easiest way to make these is to put all the ingredients into a blender and blitz. But if you do mix up the batter by hand in a bowl, make a well in the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar, beat in the eggs, melted butter and milk, and transfer to a jug: it's much easier to pour the batter into the pan than to spoon it.

Step 2: Heat a smooth griddle or pan on the stove.

Step 3: When you cook the pancakes, all you need to remember is that when the upper side of the pancake is blistering and bubbling it's time to cook the second side, and this needs only about 1 minute, if that.
